Health Data Sharing Consent

Last updated: July 2026

Purpose of This Consent

This document explains what happens when you connect your Wylde Self account to a clinician's practice through the Platform. By generating or entering a share code, you consent to the data sharing described below. Please read this carefully before connecting.

How the Connection Works

Connecting to a clinician is a patient-initiated process:

  • You start it: You generate an 8-character share code in the app and share it with your clinician, or your clinician gives you a code to enter. Either way, the connection only happens with your active participation.
  • You control it: Once connected, you can revoke access at any time from the Care Team screen in the app. Revoking access stops all future data sharing immediately.
  • It's voluntary: The Wylde Self app works fully without a clinician connection. All wellness features — workouts, nutrition tracking, AI coaching, habit tracking — remain available whether or not you connect with a provider.

What Your Clinician Can See

Data shared with your clinician

When connected, your clinician's dashboard displays:

  • Daily check-ins: your reported mood, energy, sleep quality, and notes
  • Protocol adherence: whether you completed assigned protocols (medications, supplements, routines)
  • Wylde Score: your composite wellness score and its component breakdown
  • Biometric trends: weight, body metrics, and health data synced from HealthKit
  • Messages: conversations exchanged between you and your clinician through the Platform
  • Protocol details: the protocols your clinician has assigned to you and your progress

Data NOT shared with your clinician

The following remain private to you, even when connected:

  • AI coach conversations: your chats with the Wylde Self AI coach
  • Future vision images: AI-generated visualization images you create
  • Personal journal entries: private reflections and notes
  • Detailed workout logs: individual exercise sets, reps, and workout history
  • Detailed meal logs: individual food entries and meal photos

Data Security

Data shared with your clinician is protected by the same security measures that protect all Wylde Self data:

  • Encrypted in transit using TLS
  • Stored in a database with Row Level Security (RLS) — your clinician can only access data from patients who have explicitly connected with them
  • Access is logged and auditable
  • Clinicians authenticate with their own secure accounts

Risks of Electronic Data Transmission

While we implement strong security measures, you should be aware that:

  • No electronic data transmission or storage system is 100% secure
  • Data transmitted over the internet may be subject to interception, though we use encryption to minimize this risk
  • Unauthorized access, while unlikely given our safeguards, cannot be absolutely guaranteed against
  • Your clinician's use of the data on their end is subject to their own privacy practices and professional obligations

Revoking Consent

You may revoke your consent and disconnect from your clinician at any time:

  1. Open the Wylde Self app
  2. Navigate to the Care Team screen (from your profile)
  3. Tap "Disconnect" to end the data sharing relationship

When you revoke access:

  • Your clinician will no longer receive new data from your account
  • Historical data that was previously shared may be retained in your clinician's records in accordance with applicable recordkeeping requirements
  • You can reconnect at any time by generating a new share code

Acknowledgment and Agreement

By generating or entering a share code to connect with a clinician, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read and understand what data will be shared with your clinician
  • You are voluntarily choosing to share your health data
  • You understand you can revoke access at any time
  • You understand the risks associated with electronic data transmission
  • You understand that the app functions fully without a clinician connection

For questions about data sharing or this consent form, contact us at privacy@wyldeself.com.
