BPC-157

Healing Recovery Injury Protection

BPC-157 is a synthetic peptide derived from a protein found in gastric juice, shown to dramatically accelerate healing of tendons, ligaments, muscles, and gut tissue. It works by upregulating growth factors and promoting angiogenesis, making it the go-to protocol for active individuals recovering from chronic or acute injuries.

TB-500

Wound Healing Flexibility Inflammation

TB-500 is a synthetic version of Thymosin Beta-4, a naturally occurring protein that plays a key role in tissue repair, cell migration, and anti-inflammatory response throughout the body. It is particularly valued for improving range of motion, reducing systemic inflammation, and accelerating recovery from muscle tears and joint damage.

Tesamorelin

Growth Hormone Fat Loss Cognitive Function

Tesamorelin is a GHRH analogue that stimulates the pituitary gland to release growth hormone in a natural, pulsatile manner, making it one of the most effective peptides for reducing visceral fat and improving body composition. Emerging research also supports its role in enhancing cognitive clarity and verbal memory, particularly in adults over 40.

Ipamorelin

GH Release Lean Muscle Anti-Aging

Ipamorelin is a selective growth hormone secretagogue that triggers GH release with minimal impact on cortisol or prolactin, making it one of the cleanest GH-stimulating peptides available. It supports lean muscle development, improved sleep quality, accelerated fat metabolism, and is widely used in anti-aging protocols for its favorable side-effect profile.

Thymosin Alpha-1

Immune System T-Cell Regulation

Thymosin Alpha-1 is a naturally occurring thymic peptide that modulates and strengthens the immune system by enhancing T-cell differentiation, maturation, and activity. It is used clinically for immune deficiencies, chronic infections, and post-illness recovery, and is increasingly adopted by high-performers for baseline immune resilience and inflammation control.

SEMAX

Cognitive Enhancement Memory Mood Performance

SEMAX is a synthetic analogue of ACTH that increases BDNF expression in the brain, leading to measurable improvements in focus, memory consolidation, and stress resilience. Originally developed in Russia for neurological rehabilitation, it is now widely used as a cognitive enhancer and mood stabilizer in performance-oriented wellness protocols.
